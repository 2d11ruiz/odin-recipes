Lasagna

Description

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  • Spoon meat sauce on casserole dish
  • Add 4 lasagna noodles
  • Spread cheese
  • Add meat sauce
  • Repeat 2 times
  • Top with cheese
  • Heat in oven
